Riverside Italian Dining

Our Story

Italian Soul,
Quayside Heart

Sitting proudly on Newcastle's famous Quayside since 2001, Sambuca has been serving hearty, authentic Italian food at honest prices for over two decades. With views of the iconic Tyne Bridge and Swing Bridge, there is no better place to enjoy a plate of pasta or a wood-fired pizza in the city.

Part of a family of restaurants across the North East, but the Quayside location has something no other branch has: the view. Whether you are here for a leisurely lunch watching the river, a lively Friday night with live music and cocktails, or a family celebration over sharing boards and tiramisu, Sambuca always delivers.

Budget-friendly, hearty, and unapologetically Italian. That is what we do.
