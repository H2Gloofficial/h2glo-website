What is working

Midsommar is the only Scandinavian-inspired bakery in Newcastle. That is a massive differentiator. The Grainger Market location puts you in a high-footfall area. Your product is genuinely beautiful and unique. Swedish fika culture is trending. You have all the ingredients for a strong brand.

The opportunity

2,835 Instagram followers for a bakery this unique is a fraction of what it could be. Bakery content is one of the highest performing categories on TikTok and Instagram right now. Sourdough videos, pastry making, morning routine content, seasonal specials. The visual nature of what you do is perfectly suited for short-form video.

There is no Scandinavian bakery content coming out of the North East. You would own that space completely. No competition. First mover advantage.

Content Ideas

What we would create

These are formats that are performing right now for bakeries on TikTok and Instagram. All of them play to Midsommar's strengths.

01

"Morning at Midsommar"

A calming 30-second video of the bakery opening up. Dough being shaped. Coffee being poured. The Scandinavian simplicity. This is ASMR bakery content that performs incredibly well.

02

Seasonal pastry reveals

Each new seasonal item gets a dramatic reveal video. Cinnamon buns in autumn. Semlor in spring. The seasonal rotation is built-in content.

03

"What is fika?"

Educational content about Swedish coffee culture. This is the type of content that gets saved and shared because people learn something new.

04

Grainger Market walk-through

Walking through the market to find Midsommar. Showing the journey. The market itself is a content backdrop that adds character.

05

Dough process videos

Sourdough stretching, laminating, shaping. These are some of the most-watched bakery videos on the internet. Pure satisfaction content.

06

"Best bakery in Newcastle?"

Using the question format that drives comments. Controversial, engaging, gets people talking. The algorithm loves debate.
