Our Story

Light, Fluffy,
Jiggly Perfection

Souffle Baby is Newcastle's home for authentic Japanese souffle pancakes. Each pancake is carefully crafted to achieve that signature light, airy, jiggly texture that has taken the world by storm.

Our menu goes beyond pancakes. We also serve Korean bingsu (shaved ice desserts), a full range of bubble tea flavours, matcha lattes, and specialty coffee. Whether you are craving something sweet, refreshing, or cosy, we have you covered.

We offer a gluten-free and lactose-free base for our souffle pancakes, so everyone can enjoy the experience.
