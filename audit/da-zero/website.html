Our Story

Authentic Italian,
From Scratch

Da-Zero is a family-run Italian restaurant in the heart of Chester-le-Street, where everything is made from scratch. That is what "Da Zero" means, and it is the principle behind everything we do.

From hand-stretched pizza dough and slow-simmered sauces to fresh pasta made daily, we bring the authentic flavours of Italy to County Durham. Our menu features traditional recipes passed down through generations, prepared with care and served with warmth.

Whether you are joining us for a weeknight dinner, a weekend lunch with family, or ordering takeaway for a night in, you will taste the difference that fresh, from-scratch cooking makes.
