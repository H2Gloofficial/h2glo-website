Our Story

Real Charcoal,
Real Korean BBQ

INSA is Newcastle's authentic Korean BBQ experience, right in the heart of Jesmond. We bring the tradition and excitement of interactive table grilling to the North East, using real charcoal for that unmistakable smoky flavour.

Our menu features premium meats including Wagyu A5 beef, marinated bulgogi, and tender pork belly, all grilled at your table by you and your guests. Alongside our BBQ, we serve traditional Korean dishes like bibimbap, japchae, and a selection of banchan.

Every recipe follows traditional Korean cooking methods, using premium ingredients sourced for quality and authenticity.
