Where The Opportunity Is
Five Areas With Real Potential

Cakepops et al already has the product, the craft and a warm community. The gap is reach, and the kind of content that actually spreads. These are the five areas where focused effort would turn a beautiful but small account into the name every parent, couple and planner in the North East thinks of for a cake.

1

Turn the Cakes Into Video, Not Static Photos

Most posts are a beautiful finished cake, and they sit at 10 to 20 likes. The format that actually spreads is motion: the final smoothing of the buttercream, the drip going on, the first slice, the satisfying reveal. Your best-performing posts are already the videos and the "trust the process" builds, the account is telling you what to do.

We would make the reveal and the process the core format: short, satisfying, hook-first videos of a cake coming together and the big reveal at the end. Same cakes you are already making, filmed properly. This is the single biggest unlock for reach, and it costs nothing extra to capture while you bake.

2

Capture Reactions at Handovers and Markets

The thing that truly sells a cake is the moment someone sees it. The birthday child spotting their Bluey cake, a customer collecting a custom order, the dessert table reveal at a wedding. Reaction is the most shareable thing in food content, and you are at markets and events constantly with the perfect chance to film it.

We would make reaction a regular format: film customers receiving their cake, capture the faces, the gasps, the "it is perfect". That is the content that makes someone watching at home think "I want them to do mine". It is free to capture at things you are already doing.

3

Put Judith and the Bake Front and Centre

People follow people. You already used the line "POV: you found a baker that gets the assignment", and that personality is exactly what builds a following. A baker who clearly loves the craft is far more compelling than another product shot.

A regular Judith series, decorating ASMR, how a custom order works, the 24-hour turnaround in action, a day at the market, would build real connection and trust. This is some of the cheapest content to make and, based on what is performing, some of the most effective.

4

Own TikTok and Lean Into Trends

Cake TikTok is enormous, decorating, reveals, "rate this cake", satisfying ASMR, and trend-led formats. Your TikTok is linked but barely used, which is wide open ground. The same reveals and process videos, cut for TikTok with trending audio, are exactly the content that platform rewards.

Posting reaction, reveal and trend content consistently and cross-posting to Reels and YouTube Shorts would multiply your reach for no extra filming. One reveal landing on the For You page could put you in front of tens of thousands of local people overnight.

5

Reach Is the Ceiling, and Two Big Moments Are Coming

Everything points to the same thing: the product converts, there just is not enough of it reaching enough people. And you have two genuine launch moments on the way, the new store and the national NMTF Grand Final in Stratford-upon-Avon. Filmed properly, those are content gold: a build-up series, the journey, the reveal.

There is also no website yet, so every order goes through DMs. A simple site with your gallery and an order form would capture enquiries that currently slip away, and gives any ads somewhere to send people. Reach plus a clean way to order is how you turn followers into bookings.

Ready-Made Content
4 Video Ideas You Could Film This Week

These are formats working right now on TikTok and Instagram Reels, tailored to your cakes, your markets and you. They are reveal-led and reaction-led and built to spread. Any one of these could be filmed on a phone this week.

Idea 01
"A birthday cake, start to finish in 20 seconds"
Process Reveal Satisfying

Film one custom cake coming together: stacking, crumb coat, the buttercream smooth, the drip, the final details, then the finished reveal. Fast cuts, satisfying close-ups, a strong hook on the first frame ("watch this Bluey cake come to life"). Trending audio. Under 25 seconds.

Why it works: Process plus reveal is the most reliable cake format on the planet, and it is what your own top posts already are. Satisfying, rewatchable, and endlessly repeatable with every cake you make.
Idea 02
"Filming the birthday kid seeing their cake"
Reaction Joy Handover

At a handover or market pickup, film the moment the child or customer sees their custom cake for the first time. The gasp, the smile, the "it is perfect". Quick, warm, real. End on the finished cake. Warm trending audio. Under 20 seconds.

Why it works: Pure joy sells a cake better than any styled photo. This is the reel a parent watches and instantly books you for their own child's party. Reaction content consistently outperforms polished shots, and it is free to capture.
Idea 03
"POV: you found a baker that gets the assignment"
Owner Trend Personality

Your own caption, made into the hero video. Judith to camera or hands-on, taking a tricky brief and nailing it: the reference photo versus the finished cake, the 24-hour turnaround, the detail. Confident, fun, a little cheeky. 20 to 40 seconds.

Why it works: You already wrote the perfect hook. Owner-led, trend-led content builds trust and personality, and people buy cakes from a baker they feel they know. It also shows off the custom skill that sets you apart.
Idea 04
"Rate this cake, at the market"
Reaction Challenge Market

At a market, film customers tasting a cake pop or macaron and reacting, or guessing the flavour, or rating it out of ten. Capture the faces and the queue. Fast cuts, captions on their reactions. Trending audio. Under 30 seconds.

Why it works: Reaction plus a little challenge is the most shareable food format, and you are at markets anyway. Real people, real reactions, the buzz of a stall that sells out. Exactly the low-effort, high-reach content that grows a small account fast.
Digital Enhancements
Beyond Social Media

A few extra digital wins that would complement the content work.

Activate TikTok and Double Your Reach

Your TikTok is linked but barely used, and it is currently the best platform for reveal-led, satisfying, reaction-led cake content. Every video you make for Instagram can be cross-posted, so it is free reach you are leaving on the table.

  • Properly activate the @cakepops_et_al TikTok and post consistently
  • Cross-post every reveal and reaction to TikTok and YouTube Shorts
  • Use Durham, Newcastle and North East location tags, plus cake and baking hashtags

Build a Simple Website and Order Form

Right now every order goes through DMs, which means enquiries get missed and there is nowhere to send people who find you. A clean, simple website with your cake gallery and an order form would capture more orders, look the part, and give your content and any ads a proper place to land.

  • A simple, beautiful site: gallery, the range, your story, the 24-hour promise
  • An order or enquiry form: occasion, date, design, servings, contact
  • Markets and store details, so people know where to find you in person
  • Built to be the link in your bio, replacing the DM-only flow

Why it fits Cakepops: A custom-cake business lives or dies on people being able to order easily. A simple site turns "I will DM them later" (and forget) into an order placed there and then.

Run Local Ads Around Birthdays and Events

Once the content and the site are in place, a small, well-targeted local ad budget puts your best reveal videos in front of nearby parents and people planning events. One custom cake or dessert table order easily covers the spend.

  • Target local parents, couples and event planners across Durham, Newcastle and surrounds
  • Use your best reveal and reaction videos as the ads, the same content that performs organically
