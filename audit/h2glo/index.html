Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Tomorrow

These are formats that are working right now for cleaning businesses on TikTok and Instagram. All of them play to H2Glo's strengths as a visual, transformation-based service.

Idea 01
"Before and after: this driveway had not been cleaned in 10 years"
Time-lapse Transformation Viral Format

Set up a camera at a fixed angle. Film the entire driveway cleaning from start to finish. Speed it up into a 30-60 second time-lapse. Show the dramatic transformation. End with a clean, gleaming driveway and a text overlay with your name and area.

Why it works: This is the single most viral format in the cleaning niche on TikTok. Before/after time-lapses of neglected driveways regularly get millions of views. The transformation is so dramatic that people watch it on repeat, share it with friends, and save it. It is the ultimate "I need to hire these people" content.
Idea 02
"POV: You hired H2Glo for your patio"
POV Experience Satisfying

First-person footage of the team arriving at a property, unloading equipment, and setting up. Then the satisfying footage of the patio being cleaned section by section. Build the anticipation. End with the big reveal of the completed patio, looking brand new.

Why it works: POV content puts the viewer in the customer's shoes. They imagine what it would be like to hire you. The satisfying reveal at the end creates an emotional response that drives saves, shares, and DMs asking for quotes. It makes the booking feel like something they deserve.
Idea 03
"The difference professional cleaning makes"
Split Screen Comparison Simple

Film a surface that is half cleaned and half dirty. The camera slowly pans across the dividing line. The contrast speaks for itself. No voiceover needed. Just a trending audio track and the striking visual difference between the two halves.

Why it works: Side-by-side comparison content is one of the simplest formats to create but one of the most effective. The visual contrast is so striking that it stops people scrolling. It is incredibly shareable because it needs no explanation. People tag friends and say "we need this for our drive."
Idea 04
"How we clean block paving from start to finish"
Educational Process Trust Building

Full process video showing every step: initial assessment, pressure washing, chemical treatment, resanding the joints. Show the expertise behind the service. Explain briefly why each step matters. End with the finished result looking immaculate.

Why it works: Educational content builds trust and positions H2Glo as the expert. People learn that professional cleaning is more than just a pressure washer and a hose. It justifies the price, shows the value, and makes viewers feel confident that you know what you are doing. It also performs well with the algorithm because watch time is high.
Idea 05
"Customer reaction when they see their driveway"
Reaction Emotional Social Proof

Film the homeowner seeing the finished result for the first time. Their genuine surprise and delight at the transformation. "Oh my God, it looks brand new." These real, unscripted moments are pure gold on social media.

Why it works: Genuine customer reactions are the most powerful social proof there is. When a viewer sees a real homeowner genuinely shocked at how good their driveway looks, it creates an emotional connection that no amount of professional marketing can replicate. It drives engagement, builds trust, and makes viewers want the same experience for themselves.

Beyond Social Media

These are strategic changes that would make a real difference alongside the social media work. Each one is a growth lever in its own right.

Optimise Your Google Business Profile

You have a Google Business Profile which is a great foundation. The next step is making it work as hard as possible. Upload before/after photos of every job, keep hours and services updated, and build up your review count. A well-maintained Google profile is the difference between appearing first in local searches and being buried below competitors.

  • Create the profile with your correct business name, phone number, and service area
  • Add before/after photos of your best work to the profile
  • Start asking every happy customer to leave a Google review
  • Post weekly updates showing recent jobs and seasonal offers

Quick win: This is free and takes less than an hour to set up. But it is the difference between being invisible and being the first result someone sees. Every day without it is a day where ready-to-book customers are finding competitors instead.

Get on Checkatrade

Checkatrade is one of the most trusted platforms for finding tradespeople in the UK. Verified reviews from real customers on Checkatrade carry enormous weight with homeowners. Your competitors are on there already. Walker and Son Exterior Cleaning, for example, has a verified Checkatrade profile with reviews. Getting listed would put H2Glo on a level playing field and build credibility fast.

Many homeowners specifically go to Checkatrade when they need exterior cleaning done. If you are not on there, those customers will never find you. The investment pays for itself with the first booking it generates.

Build a Before/After Portfolio

Every single job H2Glo completes is a piece of content and a sales tool. Start photographing every project: a quick "before" photo when you arrive and an "after" photo when you leave. Build a gallery on your website and use these images across all your social channels.

  • Take consistent before/after photos at every job, ideally from the same angle
  • Organise them by service type: driveways, patios, fascias, block paving
  • Use them on your website, Instagram, Facebook, and Google Business Profile
  • Every completed job becomes a visual testimonial that sells the next one

The bottom line: A portfolio of 20-30 stunning before/after transformations is the most powerful sales tool you can build. It costs nothing but a few seconds with your phone at each job. Six months from now, that gallery will be generating enquiries on autopilot.
