Our Story

Riverside Views,
Brilliant Brunch

Riverview Kitchen is a riverside cafe with panoramic views of the River Wear in the heart of Durham. Perched on Silver Street, we offer the perfect spot to watch the world go by while enjoying freshly prepared breakfast and brunch.

We operate walk-in only with fast turnaround, so there is no need to book. Just turn up, grab a table, and let us take care of the rest. We are proudly dog-friendly too, so your four-legged friends are always welcome.

From towering pancake stacks to classic full English breakfasts, everything is made fresh to order using quality ingredients.
