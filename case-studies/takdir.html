The approach

Lead with the people, not the product

Takdir came to us wanting to build a presence from the ground up. We started their TikTok from zero followers, with no existing audience to lean on.

Rather than polished ads, we put the staff front and centre. The people who actually cook and serve the food became the faces of the content. That created a genuine sense of the vibe of the restaurant and the community around it, the kind of personality you cannot fake and competitors cannot copy.

What we did

Personality, offers, and standout dishes

  • Built the TikTok account from scratch and grew it alongside Instagram.
  • Staff-led content that showed the real people and the atmosphere of the place.
  • Highlighted Takdir's takeaway offers to drive real, local custom.
  • Showcased their unique meals and signature menu items as scroll-stopping hooks.
"People follow people. Putting Takdir's team on camera built a personality the whole community could connect with, and the numbers followed."

Fusion Creative

The results

A standing start to almost 4 million views

In the first month, starting from zero followers on TikTok, Takdir gained over 1,200 followers and almost 4 million views across Instagram and TikTok.

Their top video alone reached more than 1.8 million views, and the content reached over 1.27 million accounts in the period, putting Takdir's food, offers, and team in front of a huge local and national audience in a matter of weeks. From nothing to a genuine, engaged following, fast.
